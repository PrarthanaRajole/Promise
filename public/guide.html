What Are Stocks?

Stocks represent partial ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you're essentially owning a piece of that company and are entitled to a portion of its assets and earnings.

Why Should You Invest?

  • To grow your wealth over time
  • To beat inflation
  • To meet life goals (education, travel, retirement, etc.)
